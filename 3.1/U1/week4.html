Compound Subjects & Predicates

▾
Compound subject — two or more subjects joined by and or or.
Compound predicate — two or more verbs joined by and, but, or or.
SentenceTypeExplanation
Harsh weather and climate make it hard to live in the desert. Compound Subject weather and climate joined by and
