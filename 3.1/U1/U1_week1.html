Grammar: Simple Sentences

▾
A simple sentence has a subject and a predicate (verb).
The subject and verb must agree — singular subject → singular verb; plural subject → plural verb.
Sentences begin with a capital letter and end with . ? !
Subject (who/what)VerbSimple sentence
Claysleep → sleepsClay sleeps late.

Spelling: Syllable Pattern VC/CV

▾

The first vowel usually has a short vowel sound because it is closed in by a consonant.

Vocabulary Words 1–15

▾
absent 缺席的
(adj) not present at school or work
basket 籃子
(n) a container used to carry things; a point scored in basketball
comfort 安慰
(v) to help someone feel less sad or worried; (n) a feeling of ease, safety, or relaxation
cosmic 宇宙的
(adj) related to the stars, planets, or universe; extremely large or amazing
compact 小巧的；緊密的
(adj) small in size but complete
disgust 厭惡；反感
(v) to make someone feel sick or upset; (n) a strong feeling of dislike
fantastic 極好的
(adj) extremely good or exciting; hard to believe but fun
forget 忘記
(v) to not remember something
lesson 課程
(n) a period of time for learning; knowledge learned from experience
mustard 黃芥末
(n) a yellow sauce used on food
napkin 餐巾；餐巾紙
(n) paper or cloth used while eating
picture 圖片；照片；想像
(n) a photo or drawing; (v) to imagine something in your mind
silver 銀；銀色
(n) a shiny gray metal; a shiny gray color
subject 科目
(n) a topic studied in school
traffic 交通
(n) vehicles moving on roads

Academic Vocabulary (FT)

▾
competition 競賽；競爭
(n) the act of trying to win something — Jack joined a swimming competition last weekend.
solve 解決
(v) to find the answer to a problem; to fix a problem — David worked carefully to solve the math question.
custom 習俗；海關
(n) something that people have done for a long time; the place at the airport where bags are checked
occasion 場合
(n) a special time or event — A wedding is a happy occasion for the family.
organization 機構；組織
(n) a group formed to work together; the process of putting things in order

High-Frequency Words

▾
table 桌子；表格
(n) a piece of furniture with a flat top and legs; a chart that shows information in rows and columns
north 北邊；北方
(n) the direction toward the top of a map; (adj) related to cold weather or regions in the north
